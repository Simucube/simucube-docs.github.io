## Required items to make use of Co-Pedal

For single ActivePedal system with one or two Co-Pedals:

* **1 - 2 Co-Pedals**
* **ActivePedal Primary set or ActivePedal Pro**
* A fully functioning simracing system
	* A sturdy sim racing rig
	* Gaming computer with an available USB port
	* Steering wheel

For systems with total of two ActivePedals and Co-Pedal:

* **Co-Pedal**
* **ActivePedal Primary set**
* **ActivePedal Add-on set**
* A fully functioning simracing system
	* A sturdy sim racing rig
	* Gaming computer with an available USB port
	* Steering wheel
	
## Optional items

* ActivePedal Base plate. This may ease installation on rig and improve ergonomics.
