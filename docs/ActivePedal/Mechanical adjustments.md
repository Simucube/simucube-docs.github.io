## Pedal face height

To change height of pedal face

1. Loosen, don’t remove, the two (2) screws fixing the pedal, one on each side
2. Adjust the pedal height
3. Tighten the fixing screws

![](assets/pedal%20face%20vertical%20adjustment.png)

## Pedal face angle

To alter pedal face angle

1. Loosen, don’t remove, the four (4) fixing screws, two on each side
2.  Set the pedal angle
3. Tighten the fixing screws

![](assets/pedal%20face%20angle%20adjustment.png)

## Pedal offset

The easiest way to change pedal offset is done through True Drive configuration, see [Travel range](Travel%20range.md). To go beyond software configurable travel, loosen the two screws on pedal rod and move the sliding parts to wanted positions.

![](assets/pedal%20offset%20adjustment.png)

## Maximum force and travel

ActivePedal has two mechanical settings for different purposes. Choose the mode according to your required force and travel ranges.
![](assets/force%20and%20travel%20range.png)

To change the setting, follow the illustrated procedure and finally update the setting under [ActivePedal hardware configuration dialog](Software.md) in True Drive.
